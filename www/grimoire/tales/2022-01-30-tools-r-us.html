    Tools‘R’us

    Tools‘R’us

    Sunday, January 30, 20229 min read

    We’re supposed to restrict ourselves to the following tools in this CTF:

    • dirbuster
    • Hydra
    • Nmap
    • Nikto
    • Metasploit

    It’s been a little while since I had the chance to use some of these, so let’s find out how rusty I am!

    Narrative

    The target is at 10.10.28.154. Visiting http://10.10.28.154 reveals a “down for maintenance” page with no other links, but with the cryptic promise that “[o]ther parts of the website is [sic] still functional…”
